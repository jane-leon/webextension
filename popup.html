Netflix Movie Info

How to use:

  1. Navigate to Netflix.com
  2. Click on the "More Info" section of a movie or TV show
  3. Scroll down to the "About" section of the page
  4. Wait for the info panel to appear
  5. View ratings, awards & reviews!

What the extension does:

  • IMDb & Rotten Tomatoes ratings 🍅
  • Awards & recognition info 🏆
  • Box office performance 💸
  • Top 3 user reviews 👥
